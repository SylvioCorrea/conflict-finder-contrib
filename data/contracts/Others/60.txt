GENERAL CONDITIONS


1. OBLIGATIONS OF CONFIDENCE

1.1	The recipient party shall, from the date of this Agreement and for a period of five (5) years from the commencement of the Discussions:-

(a) not make, or assist or permit any other person to make, any Unauthorized Use of the Confidential Information;

(b) take all action reasonably necessary to hold and maintain the Confidential Information in strict confidence and protect it from theft, loss or disclosure, using the same degree of care that they use with respect to comparable, highly confidential information relating to their own business;

(c) restrict circulation of the Confidential Information within its own organisation to those employees who need to receive the Confidential Information in order to carry out the Discussions and to give such employees instructions to hold in confidence all Confidential Information made available to them and to use the Confidential Information only for the purposes of the Discussions;

(d) not release, sell, transcribe, transfer, publish, disclose, copy (whether by machine or by hand), display or reproduce the Confidential Information in any manner whatsoever; or assist or permit any other person to do so;

(e) not make the Confidential Information or any portion thereof, in any form or manner whatsoever, available to any other third parties, including but not limited to any other employees, directors, consultants, contractors, agents, firms, corporations, organisations, affiliates, subsidiaries, partnerships, or joint venture participants;

(f) take reasonable steps to enforce the confidentiality obligations imposed or required to be imposed by this Agreement, including diligently prosecuting at its cost, any breach or threatened breach of those confidentiality obligations by any person to whom it has disclosed any Confidential Information;

(g) on request by the disclosing party, provide and update promptly, a list of all its employees and professional advisors to whom any Confidential Information has been disclosed;

(h) upon completion of the Discussions or as otherwise requested by the disclosing party, return all Confidential Information to the disclosing party; and

(i) fully, effectually and promptly indemnify the disclosing party from all claims, actions and demands of any kind which any person may have or bring, at any time, relating to a breach of this Agreement by the recipient party.


2. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY RIGHTS

2.1	The parties acknowledge that the Confidential Information is and shall remain the sole and exclusive property of the disclosing party, and that the disclosing party owns all of the right, title and interest in the rights of intellectual property pertaining to the Confidential Information, and any improvements to the Confidential Information developed or conceived of by either party during the period set in clause 1.1, and including in relation to patents, copyrights, trade marks, designs, integrated, circuits, business reputation, trade secrets and all other intellectual property as defined by Article 2 of the Convention Establishing the World Intellectual Property Organisation of July 1967, and including the right to make application for any of them anywhere in the world.
 
2.2	Nothing in this Agreement may be construed as granting or conferring on the recipient party any propriety rights, licences or other rights in the Confidential Information.


3. SURVIVAL OF OBLIGATIONS

3.1	The parties acknowledge that:-

(a) the obligations of confidence in this Agreement will survive termination of the Discussions, for that period set out in Clause 1.1;

(b) the disclosure of any aspect of the Confidential Information will give rise to irreparable injury to the disclosing party inadequately compensable in damages. Accordingly, the disclosing party may seek to obtain specific performance or injunctive relief to prevent the unauthorized use or disclosure of the Confidential Information in addition to any other legal remedies which may be available to it, and the Parties hereby consent to the obtaining of such specific performance or injunctive relief in the event of unauthorized use or disclosure of the Confidential Information; and

(c) that the return of the Confidential Information under clause 1.1(h) does not release the recipient party from their obligations of confidentiality under this Agreement.


4. PERMITTED DISCLOSURES

4.1	Notwithstanding any provision to the contrary in this Agreement, the recipient party shall be permitted to disclose Confidential Information received from the disclosing party:-

(a) to a professional advisor of the recipient party, subject to the recipient party taking reasonable steps to ensure their advisors are fully aware of the confidential nature of the Confidential Information, before the disclosure is made; 

(b) which is required to be disclosed pursuant to a binding compulsory legal process provided that the recipient party:-

(i)	shall take all responsible steps to preserve the confidentiality of the Confidential Information, including without limitation, requesting that the Confidential Information not be released to third parties or the public;

(ii) gives the disclosing party prompt notice of the legal process so that the disclosing party may seek an appropriate protective order or pursue such other legal action necessary to preserve the confidentiality of the Confidential Information; and 

(iii)	provides reasonable assistance to and cooperates with the disclosing party in its efforts to preserve the confidential nature of the Confidential Information;

(c)	where the disclosing party has given its prior written consent to disclosure, which consent may be given or withheld in its absolute discretion; and

(d)	where the recipient party is Travel Tracker, to the Travel Tracker Company Representatives.


5. DISCLAIMER

5.1	The recipient party:-

(a) acknowledges that neither the disclosing party nor any of its professional advisers has made nor makes any representation or warranty, express or implied, as to the accuracy or completeness of the Confidential Information; and

(b) agrees that the recipient party must make their own assessment of the Confidential Information and satisfy themselves as to the accuracy and completeness of that Confidential Information.

In this Agreement, the following expressions shall have the corresponding meanings given, namely:-

Confidential Information 	all trade secrets, ideas, know-how, concepts, strategies for product development, marketing or operations, information, notes, documents, memorandums, correspondence, disks, tapes, or other materials provided to each other by the parties or as provided to the Company by any Travel Tracker Company Representatives, and whether in writing or otherwise, that:-

(a) are identified by the parties as confidential; or 

(b) would reasonably be expected by a recipient to be confidential or proprietary based on the nature of the information contained therein and the circumstances in which the materials are provided;

and including any work product or portion thereof relating to or derived from any Confidential Information, in whatever form contained, generated by the parties, which discloses Confidential Information; but exclusive of that which:-

(c) is or becomes generally available to the public through no fault or action by the recipient party; or 
(d) is in the recipient party's possession or becomes available to the recipient party on a non-confidential basis from a source, other than the disclosing party; or 

(e) is otherwise available to the general public or generally known or available to the public.

Unauthorised Use	any use that is not related to the Discussions and has not been the subject of prior written approval by the disclosing party.
