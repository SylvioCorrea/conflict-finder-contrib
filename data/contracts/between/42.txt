THIS AGREEMENT is made on                                               2005
BETWEEN:
Victorian WorkCover Authority, a body corporate established by section 18 of the Accident Compensation Act 1985 (Vic)  located at Level 21, 222 Exhibition Street, Melbourne (Authority)
[insert name and details] (Outgoing Party); and
[insert name and details] (Incoming Party)
RECITALS:
A. The Authority and the Outgoing Party entered into an Occupational Rehabilitation Provider Agreement dated [insert date], (OR Agreement) pursuant to which the Outgoing Party agreed to provide certain services to injured workers at the request of the Authority. 
B. The Outgoing Party has sought the consent of the Authority to novate its interest in the OR Agreement to the Incoming Party in accordance with the OR Agreement.  
C. The Authority wishes to approve the Incoming Party to perform the Services in accordance with the terms and conditions of the OR Agreement.
OPERATIVE PROVISIONS
1. START DATE
This Agreement shall take effect from 12.01am on [insert date] (Start Date).
2. AGREEMENT OF THE PARTIES
(a) Subject to this Novation Agreement, the Outgoing Party novates absolutely to the Incoming Party all its rights and obligations under the OR Agreement.
(b) The Authority consents to this novation and agrees to continue to perform its obligations and functions under the OR Agreement, as if the OR Agreement had been with the Incoming Party.
(c) The Incoming Party agrees that, subject to this Novation Agreement, it will be bound by the terms and conditions of the OR Agreement as if it had been a party to the OR Agreement.  
(d) The Outgoing Party shall remain liable to the Authority for all liabilities which flow from any act or omission of the Outgoing Party in the performance of its functions under the OR Agreement up to the Start Date (regardless of when that liability actually arises).
(e) The Outgoing Party shall indemnify the Incoming Party from and against any costs, claims or demands made by any person, for or in respect of the failure of the Outgoing Party to observe and perform its obligations under the OR Agreement up to the Start Date.  
3. CONSIDERATION
Each Party represents that it has received good consideration for the execution of this Novation Agreement.
4. APPROVAL
(a) Words in capitals appearing in this clause have the meanings assigned to them in the OR Agreement.
(b) With effect from the Start Date, the Authority:
(i) revokes the Approval contained in clause 2.1 of the OR Agreement; and 
(ii) approves the Incoming Party to perform the Services at the request or with the prior approval of Authorised Agents or Employers in accordance with the Act. 
(c) The Incoming Party accepts the Approval from the Authority upon the terms and conditions in the OR Agreement. 
5. COSTS AND PAYMENTS
Each party must bear its own legal costs and disbursements incurred in relation to the preparation and execution of this Novation Agreement.
6. GOVERNING LAW, JURISDICTION AND SERVICE OF PROCESS
This Novation Agreement is governed by the law in force in Victoria.
